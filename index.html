About Me

With over a decade of experience in systems engineering and infrastructure management, I've led mission-critical deployments across Fortune 500 enterprises. My career highlights include architecting the $30M United Airlines global infrastructure program spanning 200+ airports, and orchestrating a zero-downtime AWS migration for Delta Airlines' customer-facing platforms serving millions of passengers daily.

Currently pursuing my Master's degree while working as a Systems Engineer at ADB Safegate, I combine deep technical expertise with strategic leadership. My approach blends hands-on automation with enterprise-scale thinking—whether designing Kubernetes clusters for 99.99% uptime, implementing GitOps workflows that reduced deployment time by 80%, or mentoring teams through cloud transformation initiatives.

I'm passionate about building resilient systems, open-source innovation, and bridging the gap between development velocity and operational reliability.
